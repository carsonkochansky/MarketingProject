LinkedIn Integration

Connect your account for one-click posting — no pasting required

✓ Connected as
From LinkedIn Developer Portal → Your App → Auth tab
One-time setup (5 min):
1. Go to linkedin.com/developers → Create App
2. Under Auth, add your Netlify URL as an authorized redirect URI
3. Copy the Client ID here and paste the Client Secret as LINKEDIN_CLIENT_SECRET in your Netlify dashboard → Environment Variables
4. Hit "Connect LinkedIn" — after that, posting is one click forever.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?
Paid plans are billed monthly. We'll invoice you after our intro call and you can cancel any time before your next billing cycle. DIY usage is tracked monthly in your browser — it resets on the 1st of each month.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no long-term contracts on any plan. You can cancel with 30 days notice and we'll wrap up any in-progress content before your last billing date.
Do I need a Claude API key?
Only for the free DIY tier. The Co-Pilot and Full Engine plans are fully managed by our team — you don't touch any API keys or settings. Get a free Claude API key at console.anthropic.com.

Not sure which plan is right for you? Send us a message and we'll help you figure it out.
